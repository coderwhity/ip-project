TrekMate - Safety Guidelines

First Aid

  • Carry a compact first aid kit with bandages, antiseptic, and essential medicines.
  • Know basic first aid procedures for cuts, sprains, and dehydration.
  • Clean and cover any wounds immediately to prevent infection.

Weather Preparation

  • Check the weather forecast before starting your trek.
  • Dress in layers so you can adjust to temperature changes.
  • Carry rain gear, a sun hat, and sunscreen.

Hydration & Nutrition

  • Drink water regularly to stay hydrated, especially at higher altitudes.
  • Carry electrolyte packs or energy snacks for long treks.
  • Purify water from natural sources before drinking.

Trail Safety

  • Stay on marked trails and avoid shortcuts.
  • Trek with a group and inform someone about your trekking plan.
  • Respect nature: do not disturb wildlife and leave no trace behind.
